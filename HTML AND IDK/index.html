My Roundabout Design

A Promotional Web Page for Tuguegarao City

Design Concept

This roundabout design was created to improve traffic safety while enhancing the aesthetic beauty of Tuguegarao City. Its circular form represents balance, unity, and the continuous flow of movement within the community.

Design Explanation

Cultural Significance

design is inspired by both mathematics and the culture of Cagayan, especially Pancit Cabagan, a dish that symbolizes identity, connection, and unity. Its circular serving and long, flowing noodles inspired the round shape and layered rings of the roundabout. Like pancit brings people together, the roundabout represents different roads and people meeting at one center the heart of the community.

Mathematical Concepts

Mathematics is used in the design through circle concepts like radius, diameter, circumference, and symmetry. The roundabout has a diameter of 22 units, so its radius is 11 units. Using the formula C = 2πr, the circumference is 22π, or about 69.08 units, showing how math helps make the design accurate and meaningful.
